About College Funding

College Funding is a nonprofit dedicated to helping students and families navigate the complex world of paying for higher education.

Our Story & Impact

Founded in 2025 by two high school students who saw a need to make college funding more accessible, College Funded has grown from a simple idea into a platform that helps students navigate the complex world of financial aid and scholarships.

We started this project because we noticed how confusing and overwhelming the college funding process can be. Instead of letting other students struggle like we did, we decided to create a resource that breaks down these complicated topics into simple, easy-to-understand information.

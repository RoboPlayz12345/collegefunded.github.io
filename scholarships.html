Debt-Free Scholarships & Cash Awards

Scholarships are debt-free money you receive to pay for college that you don't have to pay back. They are awarded by many sources and are often flexible in how they can be used.

Avoiding Scams

‚ùå Red Flags to Watch For

  • Asking for private info before you're ready to apply
  • Application fees or credit card requirements
  • Guaranteed winnings or scholarships you didn't apply for
  • "Lucky winner" or sweepstakes-based awards
  • Too easy to apply (under 5 minutes)
  • Marketing requirements or product purchases

‚úÖ Signs of Legitimate Opportunities

  • Clear eligibility criteria and application process
  • Transparent selection process and past winners
  • Professional website and organization information
  • Detailed application requirements (essays, transcripts)
  • Verified sponsor (schools, known companies, foundations)
  • Real contact information for questions
